This Is Where Adventure Begins....

 

The Cards You're Dealt....

Icarus Holmes is a starving artist who can’t pay his rent. Negotiating with his landlord sends him on an unexpected journey, and the reader’s choices guide Icarus to find out the truth of his existence—and maybe even the truth of existence itself.

About the Author

Aron Woldeselassie’s works have appeared in Twin Cities theaters since 2010. His previous works include Sarah Goldberg Has an Intercom in Her Shower, Reefer Madness 2: The Malady of Mushrooms, and Hunting Middle Class. Today, Aron serves as the head writer for Minnesota Tonight, a news-comedy show broadcast over YouTube, and which performs monthly at the Brave New Workshop in Minneapolis, MN. Icarus is Aron’s first novel.
